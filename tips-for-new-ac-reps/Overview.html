Tips for new W3C Advisory Committee Representatives

Welcome to W3C!

This is where the Web is built; helping to lead the Web to its full potential is a deeply rewarding and humbling experience.


5.1 billion people use the Web today. Your AC vote represents roughly 11 million of those people; a population the size of Paris, France.

Volunteer

Join a Working Group and volunteer for tasks.

  • It's easiest to learn by doing.
  • Ask the Chairs to be paired up with someone.
  • Attend the in-person Working Group meetings.
  • Attend the W3C Technical Plenary in person.
  • Be nice! It can be hard to remember that in the moment.

Staff Contact

Get to know your W3C Staff Contact.

  • Navigating W3C duties.
  • Resources for new members.
  • W3C Process questions.
  • W3C votes and events that are of importance.

W3C Process

Read the W3C Process document.

  • This is how W3C gets stuff done.
  • Very well written.
  • You will refer to it many times.
  • It is constantly evolving.

Thank you!

Thank you for sharing your time with all of us;

  • to make the Web one of the greatest tools that humankind has,
  • to address some of the greatest challenges that face us as a society.
  • Welcome to W3C!
